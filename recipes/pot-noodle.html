Chicken and Wild Mushroom Ramen

Description

Egg noodles par-boiled (for 4 minutes)in a creamy chicken and mushroom broth.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Pot
  • 200ml Water

Steps

  1. Partially remove the foil lid before adding boiling water to the fill line.
  2. Reseal the foil lid and let sit for 4 minutes.
  3. Grab your fork and enjoy.
