Deconstructed Shepherd's Pie

Description

Locally sourced lamb and potato pie served with a lamb gravy.

Ingredients

  • Tesco's value range Shepherd's pie
  • That's literally it

Steps

  1. Remove from sleeve and then use a knife to cut holes into the plastic lid.
  2. Place in the microwave and heat at 900w for 3 minutes.
  3. Decanter onto suitable plate and enjoy.
