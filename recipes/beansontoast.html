Bean Toast Ã  la Microwave

Description

Fresh haricot beans slowly-cooked in a tomato sauce atop local artisan white bread.

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Toast
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Remove your beans from the can and place in a microwave-safe bowl. Heat at 900w for 2 minutes.
  2. Place your bread in the toaster and set it for 3 minutes
  3. Butter your toast and top with the beans ready to serve.
